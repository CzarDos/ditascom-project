üìã How to Request a Certificate

1 Go to Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your dashboard with all your certificate requests.

2 Click "New Request"

Find the blue "New Request" button in the top right corner of your dashboard.

3 Select Certificate Type

Choose from:

  • Baptismal Certificate - For baptism records
  • Confirmation Certificate - For confirmation records
  • Death Certificate - For death records
4 Fill in Details

Provide accurate information:

  • Who the certificate is for (yourself or someone else)
  • Personal information
  • Purpose of request
  • Parish where the sacrament was administered
5 Upload ID

Upload a clear photo of your valid ID for verification.

6 Submit Request

Review all information and click "Submit Request".

Make sure all information matches church records exactly. Incorrect information may delay processing.

üí≥ Payment Process

1 Review Payment Details

After submitting your request, you'll be redirected to the payment page. Review your request summary.

2 Choose Payment Method

Select your preferred payment option:

  • Credit/Debit Card
  • Online Banking
  • E-Wallet (GCash, PayMaya)
3 Complete Payment

Enter your payment details and submit.

4 Wait for Processing

Your request will be processed within 2-3 business days.

Keep your reference number safe! You'll need it to track your request.

üìú Downloading Your Certificate

1 Check Status

Wait for your request status to change to "Ready".

2 Go to My Certificates

Click the "My Certificates" button on your dashboard.

3 Download

Find your certificate and click the download button to get your PDF.

Save multiple copies of your certificate in different locations for safekeeping.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long does processing take?

Typically 2-3 business days after payment confirmation.

Can I request for someone else?

Yes! Select "Requesting for someone else" and provide the required relationship information.

What if I made a mistake in my request?

Contact the parish office immediately with your reference number.

Can I cancel a request?

Yes, but only before payment is processed. Contact the admin for assistance.

How do I restart the tour?

Click the help button (?) on any page to restart the guided tour.
